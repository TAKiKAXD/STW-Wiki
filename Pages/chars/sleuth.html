Sleuth

Basic Info

Type: Constructor

Perk

Cold Lead
R.O.S.I.E.'s Fire Rate is increased by 1.15% and Heat Generation is reduced by 0.65%.

Cold Lead +
R.O.S.I.E.'s Fire Rate is increased by 1.15% and Heat Generation is reduced by 0.65%. Every 12 damaging shots freezes all enemies within 256 tiles of the target for 3 seconds.

Abilities

R.O.S.I.E.
Deploy a mountable turret which deals **damage** physical damage per shot and fires **shots** 7 shots per second. R.O.S.I.E. has **ammo** 150 ammo.

Bull Rush
Charge forward **distance** 3 tiles, dealing **damage** physical damage to enemies and pushing them away with your shield.

D.E.C.O.Y.
Deploy a D.E.C.O.Y. which distracts nearby enemies for **time** seconds.

Class Abilities

B.A.S.E.
Place B.A.S.E. to reinforce attached structures with 60 Armor. Extends 4 segments from placement. Placed from trap wheel.

Kinectic Overload
Melee knockbacks and staggers trigger Kinetic Overload, dedaling 25 base Energy Damage to nearby enemies. After melee critical hits, increases Melee Impact Damage by 10% for 5 seconds, up to 5 stacks.
